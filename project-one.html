Mood

Creative Direction

The mood for autumn winter relates to finding a sense of calm and presence. Noticing the small things around us. Finding beauty in every day objects such as the seams of a basket ball and the shapes found in tree bark. Its time to slow down and appreciate the world around us. Finding a balance between home-life, work and leisure.

  • - Trans-seasonal dressing from season to season
  • - Classic styles with a modern take
  • - Active wear acts as day wear
  • - Feminine aesthetic
  • - Fluidity and form is gentle with subtle movement
  • - Elevated through details such as line and contrast
